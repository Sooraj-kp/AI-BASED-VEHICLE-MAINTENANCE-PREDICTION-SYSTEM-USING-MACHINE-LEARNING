About VehicleAI

An end-to-end AI-based vehicle predictive maintenance system designed to reduce failures, minimize costs, and improve vehicle reliability through machine learning.

Project Objective

Traditional vehicle maintenance relies on fixed-interval servicing, which often leads to either unnecessary early maintenance or delayed service causing breakdowns. This system uses real historical engine sensor data and service records to provide early maintenance alerts, reduce sudden vehicle failures, minimize repair costs, and improve vehicle reliability through data-driven predictions.

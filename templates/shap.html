How SHAP Works
01

Baseline Prediction

Model is run on 150 background engine samples to establish the average expected fault probability.

02

Feature Contribution

Each feature is replaced one at a time across all background samples. The average prediction shift measures that feature's Shapley value.

03

Signed Attribution

SHAP values are rescaled so they sum exactly to (Prediction − Baseline). Red bars push toward fault; green bars push toward healthy.

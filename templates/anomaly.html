How Isolation Forest Works
01

Train on Normal Data Only

Trained exclusively on 7,218 normal (condition=0) engine readings — learns what "normal" looks like across all 6 parameters simultaneously.

02

Isolate Anomalies

Builds random isolation trees. Anomalous points are isolated in fewer splits (shorter path length). Normal points require more splits to isolate.

03

Score & Flag

Anomaly score 0–100 computed from path lengths. Score >50 = anomaly. Catches multi-parameter outliers invisible to single-threshold rules.
